AI video workflow

AI Labs

AI Labs helps creators generate short-form videos and send completed video files to TikTok drafts for final review and publishing in the TikTok app.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy

Service Overview

The service is designed for creator-owned workflows. Users create or select a video, authorize TikTok access, and request that the video be uploaded to their TikTok draft inbox. Final editing and publishing remain under the user's control inside TikTok.

TikTok Integration

AI Labs uses TikTok Login and TikTok's Content Posting API to upload user-requested video drafts. The integration does not publish videos automatically without the user's final action in TikTok.
