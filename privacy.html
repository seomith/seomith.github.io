Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Labs handles information when users create video content and connect TikTok for draft uploads.

2. Information We Process

AI Labs may process information you provide directly, such as video files, prompts, configuration settings, and support messages. When you authorize TikTok access, we may receive TikTok account authorization data such as access tokens and basic account information permitted by the scopes you approve.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to operate the service, generate or prepare video content, upload TikTok drafts at your request, maintain security, troubleshoot errors, and respond to support or policy questions.

4. TikTok Data

TikTok access tokens are used only for TikTok API requests initiated by the user, such as uploading a video draft. AI Labs does not sell TikTok user data and does not use TikTok authorization to publish videos automatically without user action.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers or platform APIs only as needed to operate the requested workflow, comply with law, prevent abuse, or protect the service.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law, security needs, or user-requested records.

7. User Choices

You may stop using the service at any time. You may revoke TikTok access through TikTok's account or app settings. You may contact us to ask questions about information associated with your use of AI Labs.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No online service can guarantee complete security.
